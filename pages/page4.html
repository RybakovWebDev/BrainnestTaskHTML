There are quite a lot of titles coming for PS VR2, but here are some I'm personally excited about:

Moss and Moss: Book II

A very cute series of games about a brave young mouse on an adventure to save their kingdom.

Resident Evil: Village

While ot originally a PS VR2 title, but a VR mode for it was announced just recently with support for PS VR2.
Part of a famous Resident Evil franchise, this is a survival horror game about zombies, essentially.

Horizon Call of the Mountain

A action-adventure game that is a spin-off of the Horizon series, telling a story
about the far future where Earth is overrun by machines.
